Disease which our webApp can predict are

Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic illness that develops when the body either cannot use the insulin that the pancreas makes properly or does not create enough of it. Uncontrolled diabetes frequently results in hyperglycemia, or elevated blood sugar, which over time causes major harm to numerous bodily systems, most notably the blood vessels and neurons.

Symptoms

Increased thirst

Frequent urination

Extreme hunger

Blurred vision

Heart disease

Heart disease describes a range of conditions that affect your heart. It includes Blood vessel disease, such as coronary artery disease. Heart rhythm problems (arrhythmias). Heart defects you're born with (congenital heart defects)

Symptoms

Chest pain,tightness, pressure and discomfort

Shortness of breath

Pain, numbness, weakness

Pain in the neck, jaw, throat, upper abdomen or back

Parkinson's disease

Parkinson's disease is a chronic and progressive movement disorder that affects the nervous system. Its symptoms may vary from person to person, and early signs can be mild and easily overlooked.

Symptoms

Shaking especially in the hands, arms, legs, or jaw

Slowness of movement

Muscle Rigidity

Postural Instability

Changes in speech, writing, and facial expressions

Liver disease

Your liver is your body's largest solid organ. Symptoms of liver disease may vary, but they often include swelling and pain. The most common liver disease is hepatitis. Hepatitis is a chronic condition that can affect any part of the liver, including the liver itself. Sometimes there are no symptoms.

Symptoms

yellow skin and eyes

swollen ankles, legs, or abdomen

nausea

vomiting
